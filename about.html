About Us



ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Shams Ur Rahaman Faizi is an LLC, Afghanistan-based company, was established in 2021. The company was formed with the aim of processing and exporting dried fruits and nuts and has grown to become one of the leading exporter companies in Afghanistan. The company activity will be focused on dried fruits and nuts processing, sort, and packaging, distributing (locally), and export to international markets in broader ranges.

We are committed to providing the best products with the highest standard of quality to our valued customers based on their needs and requests. In this regard, we tried to be a good intermediary between the producer and the consumers. So we are in contact with farmers and others who buy/produce our needed products directly from the cultivated area.

We take care of hundreds of our client’s needs throughout the country, as well as supply to international buyers/customers outside the country, especially Indian markets.
